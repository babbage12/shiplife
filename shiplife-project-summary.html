ShipLife

An Interactive 3D Globe Experience Documenting Life as a Cruise Ship Musician

Project Overview

ShipLife is an immersive web-based experience that brings to life the journey of a cruise ship musician across the world's oceans. Using a 3D interactive globe powered by Three.js, visitors can explore over 100 ports of call, each with rich photo galleries, personal narratives, and AI-generated porthole artwork.

The Three Chapters

The experience unfolds through three narrative "doors" that visitors must unlock to reveal the full story:

Chapter 1: Toledo, Ohio

The Beginning - Where the journey starts, from Midwest roots to maritime dreams.

Chapter 2: Darwin, Australia

The Transformation - Proving grounds in the remote Australian north, where musicians become mariners.

Chapter 3: Auckland, New Zealand

The Arrival - Finding home at the edge of the world, where the Pacific becomes familiar.

Key Features

  • Interactive 3D Globe - Rotatable Earth with clickable location markers using Three.js WebGL rendering
  • AI-Generated Porthole Art - Each location features unique AI-generated artwork displayed in a ship's porthole frame
  • Rich Content Panels - Expandable panels with hero images, demographics strips, story sections, and photo galleries
  • Lightbox Navigation - Full-screen image viewer with previous/next navigation through galleries
  • Progress Tracking - localStorage-based tracking of visited locations and unlocked chapters
  • Responsive Design - Adapts from desktop to mobile with touch gesture support
  • Alphabetical Sidebar - Searchable location list organized by name
  • Custom Icon System - 40+ unique SVG symbols representing location themes (heritage, paradise, adventure, culture)
