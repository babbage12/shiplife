About Šibenik

Medieval Croatian city on the Adriatic coast. Known for the UNESCO-listed Cathedral of St. James (Katedrala sv. Jakova), built entirely of stone without mortar. Narrow stone streets, fortresses, and stunning views of the Dalmatian islands.
